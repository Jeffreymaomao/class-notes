    [$\textit{Hint}$: $10^{-{2^n}}$ converges to $0$ of order $2$.]
    sol:(Algorithm and Convergence)
    $\{p_n\}\to p$ of order $\alpha$ if there is an asymptotic error constant $\lambda>0$ such that \[\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\abs{p_{n+1}-p}}{\abs{p_n-p}^{\alpha}}=\lambda.\] Let $p_n$ be $p_n=x^{-2022^n}$, where $x>0$ \[\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\left|x^{-2022^{n+1}}\ \right|}{\abs{x^{-2022^n}}^{\alpha}} =\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\abs{x^{-2022^{n}\times2022}}}{\abs{x^{-2022^n}}^{\alpha}}\color{red}{\quad\rm (5pts)}\] Taking $\alpha=2022$, the limitation become \[\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\abs{x^{-2022^{n}\times2022}}}{\abs{x^{-2022^n}}^{2022}} \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\abs{x^{-2022^{n}}}^{2022}}{\abs{x^{-2022^n}}^{2022}} =1>0,\] So for any $x>0$, the order of convergence of sequence $p_n=x^{-2022^n}$ is $2022$.
